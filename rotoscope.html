📝 Notes

Production Notes

🎯 Project Goal

Create a 12-week rotoscope animation series tracing real footage frame-by-frame to master the fundamentals of rotoscoping.

⚙️ Software Used

Adobe After Effects for rotoscoping, with additional compositing and color grading in the later weeks.

⏱️ Time Investment

Each weekly assignment took approximately 4-6 hours of frame-by-frame tracing, cleanup, and rendering.

📚 Learning Outcomes

Mastered frame-by-frame animation, edge refinement, timing, and compositing integration through practical rotoscope exercises.
